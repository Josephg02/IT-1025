How to obtain a CompTIA Sec+ Certification

The CompTIA Sec+ certification is a must have if you want to get your foot in the door for cyber security.
To get the certification you should first prepare by brushing up on CompTIA Security+ exam objectives
and exam practice questions.The test itself has 90 questions and has a time limit of 90 minutes to complete.
To pass the exam and recieve the certifcation you need a passing score of 750 on a scale of 100-900.
